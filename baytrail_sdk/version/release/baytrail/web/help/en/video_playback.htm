On the Playback tab page, you can play back, clip, and download a recording.
To play back a recording:

1. Select a date. Note that a blue date indicates that records are available on this date.

2. Click Search.

3. Select a start time on the timeline. For the timeline, you can zoom in or out.

4. (Optional) Enter a time under Go to and click the icon next to the time. The time will be configured as the start time.

5. Click Play.

—You can drag the timeline to change the start time.

—You can move your cursor to a point on the timeline to show the time accurate to the second of the point.

—If you click a point on the timeline, the time of the point will be displayed in the center.

The following table provides the description for each option.
/ Start or pause a playback.
Stop a playback.
Slow the playback down. Clicking the button will decrease the playback speed to 1/2x and clicking the button again will decrease the speed to 1/4x.
Accelerate the playback. Clicking the button will double the playback speed. The highest speed is 8x.
Play the previous frame.
Play the next frame.
Play the sound.
Draw a square rightwards to digitally zoom in. Draw a square leftwards to return.
Take a snapshot. By default, all the snapshots are saved in C:\IPCWeb\Pic\PlayBack. To change the save path, see Settings--Local.
Clip a recording. Clicking the button will start a clipping and clicking the button again will stop the clipping. By default, all clips are saved in C:\IPCWeb\Video\PlayBackCut. To change the save path, see Settings--Local.
Download a record to your PC. By default, all records are saved in C:\IPCWeb\RecPlayback. To change the save path, see Settings--Local.
/ Zoom in or out the timeline.
Play back a record in full screen mode. Press Esc or double-click the playback window to return.
